dd-confirm-archiving¶

Registers the Data Vault archived status in the Vault Catalog

Purpose¶

Polling the Vault Catalog for unconfirmed dataset versions, checking whether they are archived in the Data Vault, and registering the archived status in the Vault Catalog.
